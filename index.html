📋 JG Dispatch Board — How To Use
🔄 Loading & Refreshing
The board loads live jobs from Albi and techs from T-Sheets automatically on open. Jobs are cached for 10 hours for fast reloads. Click ↻ Refresh in the top right to force a fresh pull from both systems. A banner at the top shows when cached data is being served.
📍 Appleton / Stevens Point Tabs
Switch between your two locations using the tabs at the top. Each location has its own tech lanes, job pool, and push queue. Jobs are automatically filtered by location based on their Albi location field.
🃏 Job Cards (Bottom Pool)
All unassigned jobs appear in the pool at the bottom, sorted newest to oldest by job number. Use the 🔍 Search box to filter by job name, city, status, or type. Job cards are color-coded by type:

🔵 WTR = Water Mitigation  |  🔴 FRE = Fire  |  🟢 MLD = Mold  |  🟠 CT3 = CAT3  |  ⚫ BRD = Board Up  |  🟤 CNT = Contents
↕ Drag & Drop Assignment
Drag any job card from the pool and drop it onto a tech lane to assign it. The same job can be assigned to multiple techs (e.g. for a two-man crew). Drag a job back to the pool to unassign it. The board auto-saves every assignment — if you refresh the page, your assignments for today will be restored automatically.
📱 How Techs See Their Schedule
Once jobs are assigned to a tech's lane, those jobs automatically appear at the top of their time clock as "Today's Assigned Jobs." They tap a job to clock in — no manual searching through the full job list. Changes you make here sync to their phone within seconds via live updates.
🚫 Marking a Tech Out
Click Out on any tech lane to mark them as unavailable. A popup will ask for the reason (Sick, PTO, Personal Day, No Call No Show, Injured, or Other). The AI will automatically suggest redistributing their jobs to other techs. Click ↩ Undo to restore the tech.
🤖 AI Redistribution
When a tech is marked out with unassigned jobs, the AI panel opens automatically suggesting which jobs to move to which techs based on proximity, workload, and job type. You can accept or dismiss individual suggestions, or click Accept All to apply them all at once.
📅 On-Call Tab
Click the 📅 On-Call tab to manage the monthly on-call rotation schedule. Each day has three role slots: On-Call Tech, Backup Tech, and On-Call PM — for both Appleton and Stevens Point. Use ◀ ▶ to navigate months. Click 💾 Save to persist the schedule in the browser. Click ↑ Push to T-Sheets to create all-day "On Call" schedule events in T-Sheets for every assigned day.
⚙ Settings & API Key
Click ⚙ API Key to update your Anthropic API key (used for AI redistribution). The key is stored locally in your browser and never sent anywhere except the Anthropic API. The board works fully without it — AI features are the only thing that requires it.
