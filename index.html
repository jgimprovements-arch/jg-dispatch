📋 JG Dispatch Board — Walkthrough
A typical dispatcher's morning, top to bottom. Updated 2026-04-26.
1️⃣ When you open the board
Live jobs load from Albi; techs load from the JG employee roster. Cached for 10h for fast reload — click ↻ Refresh top-right to force fresh. The Appleton / Stevens Point tabs split your two markets (separate lanes, separate pool). The board auto-saves every move and syncs in real time across dispatchers — when Brent moves a job, it appears on Hannah's screen with a "🔄 Board updated" toast.
2️⃣ Read the unassigned pool (bottom)
Every job not yet assigned shows here, color-coded by type:
🔵 WTR Water · 🔴 FRE Fire · 🟢 MLD Mold · 🟠 CT3 CAT3 · ⚫ BRD Board Up · 🟤 CNT Contents
Use the 🔍 search box to filter by name, city, status, or job number. Matches highlight in both the pool AND tech lanes — useful for "where's that Lam job?"
3️⃣ Drag jobs onto techs
Drag any card from the pool onto a tech lane to assign. The same job can land on multiple techs for team work — drop it on each one. Drag back to the pool to unassign. Reorder within a lane by dragging up/down. Cross-location jobs (Appleton job dropped onto a Stevens Point tech) work too — they appear in the cross-loc section.
4️⃣ Read the card footer for context
Each assigned card shows a small footer with two cues for scheduling decisions:
"Brent moved to Ryan · 2h ago" — last dispatcher action and who took it
"🔧 Last on-site: Mike · yesterday" — most recent tech to clock in to this job
A pulsing green dot means a tech is clocked in right now. Use the on-site line to keep techs on jobs they already know.
5️⃣ Pre-schedule for tomorrow (or any future date)
Use the date picker in the header (◀ ▶ + calendar input). Pick tomorrow → drag jobs onto techs → close out. Those assignments are stored against that date and auto-appear on the dispatch board at 5am when that date becomes today. No manual promote step. Pre-scheduled jobs show a SCHEDULED badge in the tech's app. Click TODAY to jump back. Future-date pool only shows jobs that aren't on today's lanes.
6️⃣ Mark a tech out
Click Out on any tech's lane → choose reason (Sick / PTO / Personal / NCNS / Injured / Other). The tech's lane grays out, drops are blocked. The AI panel may open with redistribution suggestions for their orphaned jobs. Click ↩ Undo to restore. Vacation requests approved through the platform auto-mark techs out on those dates — even on future-date views.
7️⃣ AI redistribute (when needed)
When a tech is marked out with assigned jobs, the AI panel suggests reassignments based on proximity, workload, and job type. Accept or Dismiss each, or Accept All. Requires API key (set via ⚙ API Key top-right; stored locally in your browser only). Board works fine without it — just no AI suggestions.
8️⃣ On-Call rotation
The 📅 On-Call tab is a monthly calendar with three slots per day per market: On-Call Tech, Backup Tech, On-Call PM. Use ◀ ▶ to navigate months. Click 💾 Save to persist. This is the dispatcher's reference; communicate the schedule to techs separately.
9️⃣ Reset Board (be careful)
The 🗑 Reset Board button wipes all assignments for the date you're viewing. Useful at end-of-day to clear the slate. Destructive — there's no undo. Only use it when you're sure. Future-date assignments and audit history are preserved.
🔟 What techs see in their app
Their schedule is locked until they clock in for the first time today. After that, today's assignments are visible — including pre-scheduled work that arrived on its date. Lunch-break clock-outs don't re-lock. Pre-scheduled jobs get a SCHEDULED badge. They tap a job to clock in to it.
📊 Need a report on hours by job?
Open Time Reports from the sidebar (TIME section). Pick a job, set a date range, get total hours, hours by tech, and a printable PDF with the JG logo. Built for billing defense and customer disputes.
